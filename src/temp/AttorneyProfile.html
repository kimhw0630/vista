Biography Experience Publications News & Insights

Jane Doe has over 20 years of experience in corporate law, specializing in domestic and cross-border mergers and acquisitions. She has successfully guided numerous Fortune 500 companies through complex transactions, regulatory challenges, and strategic restructurings. Her approach is client-focused, ensuring that every legal strategy aligns seamlessly with broader business goals.

Before joining Sterling & Partners, Jane served as General Counsel for a major technology firm in Silicon Valley, where she oversaw global compliance and IP litigation. This in-house experience gives her a unique perspective on the practical challenges faced by corporate legal departments. She is frequently quoted in the Wall Street Journal and The Financial Times regarding trends in M&A regulation.

Jane is also deeply committed to pro bono work, regularly advising non-profit organizations on governance and operational matters. She currently serves on the board of the New York Legal Aid Society.
